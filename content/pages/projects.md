This is a short list of projects I've been working on over the past few years.

## PhD

- **Thesis**: _From high dimensionality to developmental biology - understanding the evolution of cell identity in the early embryo_
  - [ccmnet](https://github.com/brickmanlab/ccmnet): Detection of causal interactions from scRNA-seq datasets
  - [Preimplantation DL models](https://github.com/brickmanlab/proks-salehin-et-al): DL models for predicting cell types in early mouse and human development ([web portal](https://huggingface.co/spaces/brickmanlab/hf-preimplantation-portal), [datasets](https://zenodo.org/records/13749348), [models](https://huggingface.co/brickmanlab))
  - [nf-core/marsseq](https://github.com/nf-core/marsseq): Preprocessing pipeline for single-cell MARS-seq experiments
  - [cat-python](https://github.com/brickmanlab/cat-python): Estimates similarity between clusters from different single-cell sequencing techniques
- Small tools and utilities are available at [github.com/brickmanlab](https://github.com/brickmanlab)
  - [brickmanlab/scanvi-explainer](https://github.com/brickmanlab/scanvi-explainer): SCANVI explainer
  - [brickmanlab/primeseq](https://github.com/brickmanlab/primeseq): PRIME-seq preprocessing nf-core pipeline
