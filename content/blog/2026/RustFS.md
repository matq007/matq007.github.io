MinIO a popular s3 compatible storage made some headlines past few month where they removed UI interface from the community license and then later switched repository to maintenance mode. This move forced FOOS community to create a fork [pgsty/minio](https://github.com/pgsty/minio) while others are now switching to [RustFS](https://rustfs.dev/binary-replacement-a-simple-way-to-migrate-from-minio-to-rustfs/).
